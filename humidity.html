Humidity


The humidity of a particular latitude doesn't appear to have a correlation as shown on this graph. There is a slight trend for a higher latitude to have a higher humidity, but generally the data points are scattered. The data points appear to form a horizontal line at the 100 y-axis mark because this is the maximum value of humidity.
